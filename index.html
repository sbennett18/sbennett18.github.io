Who am I?

I am currently working towards my Master of Science degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Colorado at Boulder and expect to graduate in May 2014. I completed my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Minor in Computer Science from the University of Colorado at Boulder in May of 2013. While studying at CU, I have held various software and hardware positions, and was/am a member of a number of research oriented projects.

Much of my experience has been in the Electrical and Software side of the Aerospace field. I believe this background has given me an advantage over many other Electrical or Computer engineers in that I have learned early on how to work and communicate with engineers of different disciplines (e.g. Mechanical, Aerospace) and work in tight environments with uncommon and rigorous requirements and deadlines.

I am very interested in working on smaller, tight-knit teams in either a Software or Hardware position, or at the intersection of the two.
